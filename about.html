About Us

SERVICIOS EL PAISANO is an office that helps customer with translation and interpretation from Spanish to English or Vice versa with all the different need of the customer that lived in the Chattanooga and surround areas. SERVICIOS EL PAISANO provide more services to the Latin community.

SERVICIOS EL PAISANO was founded in November 2005 by Santos Chavez after graduated from Chattanooga State Technical Community College with Associated of Applied Science Degree in Information System Technology. Then Carmen Hernandez came in 2009 to help. Since 2009 they have worked together for better services.
