GUATEMALAN PASSPORT

Guatemalan Adult passport:

The cost is US $65.00 and need the following:

  • Birth certificate issued by RENAP
  • (Register National of Person) and DPI
  • (Personal Document of Identification) original and copy

Minor Passport

The cost is US $65.00 (first passport or renewal) and need the following:

  • Child's birth certificate issued by RENAP (Register National of Person) original and copy
  • Birth certificate of both parents, recent (original and copy)
  • Valid passport of both parents (original and copy)
  • PI (Personal Document of Identification) for both parents (original and copy)
  • The child must be accompanied by both parents
  • If one of the parents had died, he must present a death certificate
  • If one or both parents of the minor is in Guatemala or in another state of the UNITED STATES, he / she must sign an AUTHORIZATION LETTER so that the minor obtains a passport at the Consulate.

GUATEMALAN CONSULAR IDENTIFICATION CARD

To get you ID card you will need the following:

  • PASSPORT CURRENT OR EXPIRED (ORIGINAL AND COPY)
  • CERTIFICATE OF BIRTH ISSUED BY RENAP (ORIGINAL AND COPY)
  • DPI (PERSONAL DOCUMENT OF IDENTIFICATION) ORIGINAL AND COPY OR CERTIFICATE OF DPI (PERSONAL DOCUMENT OF IDENTIFICATION) ORIGINAL AND COPY
THE COST IS US $25.00

HONDURAS PASSPORT

Requirement:

  • Honduran identity card in force.
  • If you are a woman and you want your last name to be in your passport, present the marriage certificate.
  • If the request for your passport is because it was stolen, damaged or lost, must present complaint filed with the police where the event occurred or failing to make an affidavit at the Honduran consulate where you process your passport. (The Affidavit has a value of US $ 50.00)
  • Requirements for NOT having the Identity Card but that It has been processed in Honduras with National Registry of Persons
  • At least one Honduran document that has your identity number (DIGITAL birth certificate, previous passport, driver's license, password). * The official will proceed to look for the RNP photographic record to be attached to the application.

MEXICAN PASSPORT

Requirements to obtain the passport for the first time:

  1. Appear personally.
  2. Documented proof of Mexican nationality.
  3. Present an official photo ID.
  4. Make the corresponding payment.

Requirements for passport renewal:

  1. Appear personally.
  2. Present the passport you want to renew. If the passport has an observation for lack of any documents, then you must comply with the requirements as if you processed the passport for the first time.
  3. Make the corresponding payment.

*Nota: Si el pasaporte que desea renovar fue expedido antes del 2006, deberá proporcionar toda la documentación como si fuera por primera vez.

EL SALVADOR PASSPORT

Passports for the first time (Over 18)

  1. IF YOU HAVE DUI (Unique Identity Document): Only present the current DUI. It is not mandatory to present Birth Certificate. Price: $ 60.00
  2. If you had a DUI but it is damaged, lost or expired: You must renew your DUI at the corresponding office and request your passport until you receive your new DUI. Certifications are not accepted. For more information communicate to the telephone: 1-844-736-2900. Price: $60.00
  3. If you have never had a DUI, you must submit:
    • ORIGINAL birth certificate, with marginalization (if any), not older than 2 years and in perfect condition
    • ONE of the following documents in ORIGINAL: Hand-made passport, Identity Card Personal (CIP), Certificate of Identification, Election Card, Minority Card, Driver's License of El Salvador or Social Security Card (ISSS) Price: $60.00
  4. If you have never had any document from El Salvador:
    • ORIGINAL birth certificate, with marginalities (if any), that is not more than 2 years old and in perfect condition
    • Introduce yourself with your father or mother to be your witness, who must present your DUI or valid passport in original
    • If your parents can not be found, 2 close relatives can be present to be your witnesses, who must present their DUI or valid passport in original
Total price: $80.00 (Passport: $60.00 + Identity Card to sign for witnesses: $20.00)
