2025 Tax Preparation & Filing

INCOME TAX

We prepare taxes for this year and previous years. We also prepare taxes for other states. If you are ready to prepare your taxes please bring the following documents:

  • Identification (Passport, Consular ID, Driver's License)
  • Social Security OR ITIN (Identification Number)
  • Form to be included with your tax (Form 1099. W2)

If you are going to include dependents in your taxes please have the following documentation ready:

  • Social Security OR ITIN (Taxpayer's personal identification number)
  • Birth certificate
  • Vaccination Card (If your dependent is under 5 years old)
  • Record of the school (If your dependent is 5 year and under 18)
  • Medical Insurance or Medical Insurance Form
